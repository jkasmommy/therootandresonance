Our Story

Nestled in the sacred landscape of Cortez, Colorado, The Root & Resonance was born from a deep connection to the earth and its healing wisdom. Our journey began with a simple belief: that wellness is not just about the body, but about the harmony between mind, body, and spirit.

Every tincture we craft, every tea we blend, and every sacred piece we create is infused with intention and respect for the ancient traditions of plant medicine. We source our herbs responsibly, honoring the land that provides for us.

Our sacred geometry creations, crafted with precision using xTool technology, serve as reminders of the interconnectedness of all things and the beauty found in natural patterns and divine proportion.
